The Kwajbasket project started in early 2011 as an exploration of the sonic possiblities of minimal guitar music. Over the course of several years and full length releases, the sound and aim of the project have been refined. I still record all of the sounds using a guitar into a GNX3 Digitech multi-effects processor, a Boss RC-2 loop station, an M-Audio M Track mixer, and processing in Ableton Live. How you choose to consume the music, whether actively or passively listening, is up to you. Thank you for your interest and support.
